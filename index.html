About the Project

This system uses a trained Gradient Boosting Classifier to predict crime hotspot risk levels across 700 Indian police districts. By analysing district geography, time of day, and seasonal patterns, it helps law-enforcement agencies allocate patrol resources proactively rather than reactively.

The model was trained on a dataset of 50,000 synthetic records inspired by real-world India crime data, covering major cities from Mumbai to Srinagar, encoding district-level crime rates, festive season spikes, and hour-of-day patterns with added noise for realism.
